As you approach the front yard, you will immediately be taken by the lush landscaping and majestic tree that drapes across the entrance. You will fall in love the moment you enter this bright, airy, and open floor plan. The expansive living space is stunning with 22 ft vaulted ceilings and exposed beams, skylights that flood the space with natural light, beautiful chandeliers that catch your eye and a cozy stone fireplace. The open-concept gourmet chef’s kitchen is perfectly set up with marble counters, high-end appliances, exquisite tile backsplash, and a grand center island that is sure to be the gathering space for your friends and family. Move through the fabulous barn doors to a spacious family room complete with built-ins and large sliders. The primary retreat has views of the backyard, a spa-like bathroom with marble counters, a double vanity, and a walk-in closet. The additional bedrooms offer an abundance of light and share a beautiful bathroom.

We Love:

The fantastic indoor/outdoor living! The outdoor space is an entertainer’s dream – dine alfresco under the stars while enjoying the serene fountain, grassy backyard, and perfect BBQ setup. With tons of room to run and play, the opportunities are endless!

You'll Love:

The detached, permitted ADU space offers tremendous value and opportunity. This quaint space has generous light-filled living space, a fully equipped kitchen with built-in appliances, a bathroom, and a loft for extra storage. Host your friends and family, use the space as a separate work-from-home office, or rent out for passive income!

The Neighborhood:

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city, in this wonderful neighborhood. Surrounded by wonderful restaurants, shopping, and more. So much to do is nearby, including the Westchester Golf Course, parks and skateparks, and the library. Close proximity to LAX, and a short bike ride to the Beach. This is a fabulous location to enjoy the vast offerings of the Westside!
