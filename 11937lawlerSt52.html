As you make your way up the path to the entrance, you’ll be struck by the original, one-of-a-kind architectural detail. You’ll be greeted by an open atrium that is filled with lush greenery, as you arrive at the stately glass front door. Upon entering, you will immediately feel the sense of space with ultra-high ceilings and an open light-infused living area. The entry takes you to a generous living room with hardwood floors, massive floor to ceiling windows and sliding Fleetwood doors that lead to the large patio area and grassy yard, perfect for outdoor gatherings, playing ball, or just relaxing! The centerpiece of the living room is a stunning, 12-foot high wood-burning fireplace encased in beautiful basalt tiling. Next to the living room sits the open dining area, large enough for a big holiday sit-down dinner, also overlooking the exceptional backyard and city view! The kitchen is an entertainer’s dream, with sleek black granite counters and basalt tile floors that perfectly contrast with gorgeous custom-built solid maple cabinetry. High-end appliances include a Miele stovetop with grill and a double oven. The primary bedroom is infused with natural light, with hardwood floors and glass doors that open to the patio area (and again, those incredible VIEWS!) The generous en-suite bathroom has black granite counters and a separate shower/tub. There are 2 additional bedrooms, one of which has floor-to-ceiling windows looking out to the front and atrium, currently being used as a home office. A large laundry room features tons of built-in cabinetry and counters for sorting and folding. Other unique features include hard-to-find pocket doors in several of the rooms, and custom wall molding throughout.

We love:

The enormous floor to ceiling windows allowing natural light to infuse every room. The incredible high ceilings and open living space, combined with the ultra-cool, mid-century architectural vibe make this home truly unique and remarkable. The space feels like an art piece, in the form of an ultra-functional home that’s been curated with the finest taste and design!

The Neighborhood:

Nestled in the hills of Mar Vista, yet just minutes from the amazing eateries, shops and the local farmers market. Also, in close proximity to all that Venice and Culver City have to offer. And, of course, only minutes to the beach! Everything you need is here, welcome home.
