“The Hollywood Bowl House” is a rare opportunity to own a piece of Los Angeles history designed by renowned architect David Hertz. Sited on an expansive, double lot overlooking the ocean & seconds from the sand, the exterior of this immaculate 7,100+ SF residence is comprised of original, 1919 Hollywood Bowl cedar benches, showcased amongst mature California landscaping & surrounded by custom fencing for consummate privacy. Enjoy the warmth of curated, contemporary interiors & a bright, open floor plan that seamlessly allow for the highest caliber of indoor/outdoor entertainment & wellness.

Upon entry through the striking 2-story-high front door, the first level features a large living & formal dining room/bar, gym & sauna – with multiple access points to the massive front lawn, pool/spa, outdoor shower & 6-car garage. With the ease of an in-home elevator, upstairs find 4 spacious ensuite bedrooms & multiple office/studio spaces, adjoined by a fully-decked outdoor zen garden. The sky-lit chef’s kitchen, complete with built-in breakfast nook & richly textured family room, open out into the panoramic rooftop deck with dining, fire pit & lounge – all with front-facing ocean views. As is typical of Hertz’s luxurious, yet ecologically sensitive projects – no detail has been spared. Notable features include solar electrical/thermal system, hydronic radiant heating, energy-efficient lighting, & more. Located just moments from the beach, Abbot Kinney shopping, dining & nightlife.
